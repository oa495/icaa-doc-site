ICAA

Interrogating Computational Approaches To Art is a five week class for examining the poetic computation work we make beyond our intentions, to consider how personal, historical, and social realities as well as the nature of the medium of computation might impact how our work is consumed, how meaning is being derived and what outcomes it makes possible. It has been taught at the School for Poetic Computation three times by Omayeli Arenyeka.
